Research Interests

Monitoring Systems and Robots

Short Bio

Leonardo Montecchiari received the master’s degree (summa cum laude) in computer science from the University of Bologna, Italy, in 2019. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Structural and Enviromental Health Monitoring (SEHM2) Program, University of Bologna. He is also a Co-Founder of Modal Nodes, the research and development group of Epoca srl. His current research interests include unmanned aerial/ground systems, the IoT, and edge computing.
