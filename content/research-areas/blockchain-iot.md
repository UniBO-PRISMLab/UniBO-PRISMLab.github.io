At IoT Prism Lab, we recognize the critical role that distributed ledger technologies can play in
securing machine-to-machine transactions in multi-actor scenarios. Our research focuses on the
integration of blockchain technologies with the IoT to create a more secure and reliable
monitoring system with a strong emphasis on data immutability.

In addition, we are also interested in exploring distributed Oracle-based architectures that
enable us to check the veracity of sensor data.
