Our edge computing research focuses on enabling low-latency critical applications and supporting
high-throughput data applications by offloading data processing tasks nearby their origin. We
explore architectures to facilitate data management, caching, and analytics on the edge and on
the edge-to-cloud continuum. Additionally, we investigate the application of edge AI solutions
on the extreme IoT edge, including resource-constrained devices and micro-controllers.
