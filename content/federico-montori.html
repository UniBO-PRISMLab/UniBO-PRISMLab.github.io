Research Interests

Mobile Crowdsensing and IoT Data Analytics

Short Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher since May 2019 with the University of Bologna, Italy, where I also obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering in the same year. I received the B.S. and the M.S. in Computer Science, both Summa Cum Laude, from the University of Bologna in 2012 and 2015 respectively. I have been a visiting researcher at Swinburne University of Technology, Australia, and Luleå Tekniska Universitet, Sweden. I participated in several EU projects and I am now Work Package leader for the H2020 Project Arrowhead Tools. My primary research interests include Internet of Things, Mobile CrowdSensing (MCS), context-aware computing, IoT automation and data analysis for IoT scenarios.
