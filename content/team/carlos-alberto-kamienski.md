Carlos Alberto Kamienski is a Full Professor at the Federal University of ABC (UFABC), Brazil,
and a long-standing collaborator of the IoT Prism Lab. His research spans the Internet of Things,
IoT platforms and smart-agriculture applications, including joint EU–Brazil research projects on
IoT for smart irrigation.
