Leonardo Montecchiari received the master's degree (*summa cum laude*) in Computer Science from
the University of Bologna, Italy, in 2019. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the
Structural and Environmental Health Monitoring (SEHM²) Program, University of Bologna.

He is also a Co-Founder of Modal Nodes, the research and development group of Epoca srl.

His current research interests include unmanned aerial/ground systems, the IoT, and edge
computing.
