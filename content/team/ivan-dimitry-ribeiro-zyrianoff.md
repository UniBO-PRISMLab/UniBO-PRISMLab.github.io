Ivan Dimitry received a B.S. degree in Computer Science and an M.S. degree in Information
Engineering from the Federal University of ABC, Brazil. His Master's thesis encompasses areas
such as the Internet of Things and Fog Computing.

His previous work was in the design, development, and operation of software and communication
components belonging to an IoT Platform for Smart Irrigation in a Horizon 2020 research project
between the EU and Brazil.
