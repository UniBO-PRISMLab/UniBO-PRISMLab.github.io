The IoT Prism Lab brings together faculty members, postdoctoral researchers and PhD candidates from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Bologna. Our work spans pervasive IoT systems, edge intelligence and decentralised data management across the device-to-cloud continuum.
