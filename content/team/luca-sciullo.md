Luca Sciullo received his Master Degree (*summa cum laude*) in Computer Science in 2017, from the
University of Bologna, Italy. He is a Ph.D. candidate and research fellow in Computer Science and
Engineering at the University of Bologna, Italy.

He was a Visiting Researcher at the Huawei European Research Center of Munich, Germany. He is
part of the IoT Prism Lab directed by Prof. Marco Di Felice and Prof. Luciano Bononi.

His research interests include wireless systems and protocols for emergency scenarios, wireless
sensor networks, IoT systems and Web of Things.
