The BeeDrones project aimed at designing and developing low-power monitoring systems for smart
agriculture applications based on the integration of wake-up ground sensors and drones.

### IoT Prism Lab contribution

We led the design of advanced path-planning algorithms for drones collecting data from wake-up
based sensor nodes, and realised small prototypes of the hybrid ground-aerial communication
system.
