TRACE aims to develop an innovative methodology to enhance product traceability, increase
transparency across the full production chain, and support quality assurance for medicinal and
aromatic plants cultivated in Emilia-Romagna.

The project emphasizes data-driven traceability workflows to improve product value and
trustworthiness across national and international markets.
