ZONIA-MC introduces a multi-chain, zero-trust oracle system for blockchain-enabled IoT
applications, with the goal of improving data integrity, decentralization, and interoperability.

The project targets trusted IoT data retrieval and cross-chain request resolution by selecting
the most suitable blockchain according to cost, performance, and energy-efficiency constraints.
