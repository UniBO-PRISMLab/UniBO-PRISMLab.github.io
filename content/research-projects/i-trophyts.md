I-TROPHYTS is a PRIN 2022 project focused on IoT smart-health applications for physiotherapy,
integrating humanoid robotics, IoT-based systems, and knowledge-representation techniques.

The project involves multiple Italian universities and CNR to investigate and validate advanced
technology building blocks for intelligent rehabilitation scenarios.
