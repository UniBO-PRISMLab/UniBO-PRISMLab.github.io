The SWAMP project designed and developed IoT-based methods and approaches for smart water
management in the precision-irrigation domain.

### IoT Prism Lab contribution

We contributed to the development of IoT systems for data acquisition from heterogeneous
sources, data integration and data visualisation through mobile applications aimed at the human
operators managing the irrigation process.
