SEAWALL brought academic researchers and companies together to deploy low-latency cloud
solutions supporting industrial applications with strict latency requirements.

### IoT Prism Lab contribution

We contributed to the design and implementation of an IoT architecture supporting seamless and
adaptive migration of workloads across the edge–cloud continuum, driven by the latency
requirements of the industrial scenario.
