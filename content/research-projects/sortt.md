SORTT is a two-year PRIN 2022 PNRR research project that proposes an IoT-serviced and
ontology-based remote human digital twin for physiotherapy.

The project focuses on remote supervision of rehabilitation pathways at patients' homes through
real-time monitoring, IoT and edge-computing integration, and ontology-driven knowledge
representation.
