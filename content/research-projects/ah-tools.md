The AH Tools project aimed at developing advanced new digitisation and automation tools for the
European engineering industry in several different domains.

### IoT Prism Lab contribution

We developed advanced tools for reducing the deployment costs of industrial condition monitoring
systems. We designed novel IoT platforms able to automate the full sensing → data acquisition →
data processing → actuation pipeline by integrating heterogeneous sensors and network/cloud
infrastructures through the W3C Web of Things and the Arrowhead frameworks.
