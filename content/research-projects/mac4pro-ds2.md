The MAC4PRO and DS2 projects aim at developing general-purpose monitoring platforms for the
condition assessment of industrial and civil infrastructures, leveraging the latest technologies
across civil engineering, electrical engineering and computer science.

### IoT Prism Lab contribution

We developed the **MODRON** platform for Structural Health Monitoring data acquisition,
management and analytics, with interoperability and scalability support. MODRON leverages the
W3C Web of Things paradigm to abstract away from the specifics of sensing units and to
generalise across different structures and SHM applications.
