Period

2019 – 2022 (MAC4PRO)
2021 - current (DS2)

Funding

INAIL, calls BRIC 2018 and 2020 

Project type 

Italian competitive projects 

Goal

The MAC4PRO and DS2 projects aimed at developing general purpose monitoring platform for the condition assessment of industrial and civil infrastructures leveraging the ultimate technologies provided by civil engineering, electrical engineering and computer science communities. 

Contribution(s) 

IoTPrism developed the MODRON platform for SHM data acquisition, management and analytics with interoperability and scalability support. The platform leverages the recent W3C WoT paradigm to abstract from the characteristics of sensing units and to generalize over different structures and SHM applications.
