Mobile crowdsensing simulation
This work deals with the implementation of additional features in a simulator of Mobile Crowdsensing. The simulators that we are going to consider are CrowdSenSim 2.0 (https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0140366420309361), written in C++, and TACSIM (https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10207598), written in Java. This will deal with mobility patterns, optimization algorithms and software integration.
