Period

2017 – 2020  

Funding

H2020-EUB-2017 

Project type 

European competitive project 

Goal

The SWAMP project aimed at designing and developing IoT based methods and approaches for smart water management in precision irrigation domain. 

Contribution(s) 

IoTPrism contributed to the development of IoT systems for data acquisition from heterogenous data sources, data integration, and data visualization through mobile applications for the human operators devoted to the irrigation process.
