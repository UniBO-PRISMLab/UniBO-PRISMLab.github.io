Period

2019 - 2022 

Funding

H2020-EU.2.1.1.7. - ECSEL 

Project type 

European competitive project 

Goal

The AH Tools project aimed at developing advanced new digitisation and automation tools for the european engineering industry in several different domains. 

Contribution(s) 

IoTPrism developed advanced tools for reducing the deployment costs of industrial condition monitoring systems. To this aim, we designed novel IoT platforms able to automatize the full sensing -data acquisition- data processing- actuation pipeline by integrating heterogeneous sensors and network/cloud infrastructures through the W3C Web of Things and the Arrowhead frameworks.
