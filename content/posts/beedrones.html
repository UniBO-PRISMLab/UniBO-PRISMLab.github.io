Period

2017 – 2020  

Funding

AlmaIdea 

Project type 

UNIBO competitive project 

Goal

The BeeDrones project aimed at designing and developing low-power monitoring systems for smart agriculture applications based on the integration of wake-up ground sensors and drones. 

Contribution(s) 

IoTPrism leaded the design of advanced path-planning algorithms for drones collecting data from wake-up based sensor nodes and realized small prototypes of the hybrid ground-aerial communication system.

Beedrones journal + Beedrones conference
